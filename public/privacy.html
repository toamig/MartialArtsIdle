Privacy Policy

Application: The Long Road to Heaven (package com.martialartsidle.app)
Last updated: 2 May 2026 · Effective: 2 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what data the game collects, why, and the rights you have over that data. By installing or using The Long Road to Heaven (the “Game”) you agree to the practices described here.

1. Who is responsible for your data

The Game is published by Toamig (the “Developer”, “we”, “us”). For any privacy-related question or to exercise the rights described in section 9, contact: theninjatoa@gmail.com.

The Game collects the categories of data listed below. We do not ask for your real name, email address, phone number, or precise GPS location.

3. How we use this data

  • To run, balance, and improve the Game.
  • To diagnose crashes and performance problems.
  • To show ads (rewarded video) and process in-app purchases.
  • To detect abuse (e.g. fraudulent purchase attempts, ad-spoofing).
  • To comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell your data. We do not use the data for automated decisions that produce legal effects on you.

5. Advertising and personalisation

The Game shows rewarded video ads through Google AdMob. Ads are personalised by default for users in regions where personalisation is permitted. You can reset or limit your advertising identifier at any time in your device settings:

  • Android: Settings › Privacy › Ads › Reset / Delete advertising ID.
  • iOS: Settings › Privacy & Security › Apple Advertising › Personalised Ads.

For users in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, and Switzerland, the Game presents a Google-certified consent prompt before any personalised ad is served, in accordance with the IAB Transparency & Consent Framework. You can change your choice at any time from the in-game Settings screen.

6. In-app purchases

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Google Play (Android), Apple App Store (iOS), or Steam (PC). We never see your credit-card number, full billing address, or other payment instrument data. RevenueCat receives the store-issued receipt and an anonymous user identifier so it can deliver the purchased content and protect against fraud.

7. Children

The Game is not directed at children under 13 (or under 16 where required by local law). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has played the Game and shared data with us, contact theninjatoa@gmail.com and we will delete it promptly.

8. Data retention

  • Locally-stored save data stays on your device until you uninstall the Game or clear app storage.
  • Analytics events are retained by GameAnalytics for up to 24 months from collection.
  • Ad interaction data is retained by Google AdMob according to its own policy.
  • Purchase records are retained as required by tax and consumer-protection law (typically up to 10 years).

9. Your rights

If you are located in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, Switzerland, or another jurisdiction with similar laws, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Have inaccurate data rectified.
  • Have your data erased (the “right to be forgotten”).
  • Restrict or object to certain processing.
  • Receive your data in a portable, machine-readable format.
  • Withdraw any consent you have given, at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.
  • Lodge a complaint with your national data-protection authority. In Portugal that is the Comissão Nacional de Protecção de Dados.

To exercise any of these rights, email theninjatoa@gmail.com from the device on which you played, including the anonymous user ID shown in the in-game Settings › About screen so we can locate your data. We respond within 30 days.

9.1 California residents

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and CPRA you have the right to know what personal information we collect, to request deletion, and to opt out of any “sale” or “sharing” of personal information. We do not sell personal information for monetary consideration. We do share advertising identifiers and approximate location with Google AdMob for personalised advertising; you can opt out by limiting ad tracking in your device settings (see section 5) or by contacting us.

10. International transfers

Some of our processors operate servers outside the European Economic Area, including in the United States. Where required, transfers rely on the European Commission’s Standard Contractual Clauses or an adequacy decision.

11. Security

Data in transit is protected with industry-standard TLS encryption. Local save files are stored using your device’s standard application sandbox. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security but we will notify you and the competent authority of any breach affecting your data, in line with applicable law.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when we add features, change processors, or to reflect changes in the law. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page will change accordingly. Material changes will be highlighted in-game on next launch.
