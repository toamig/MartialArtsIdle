# World

The main activity area. The world is divided into **6 major worlds**, each spanning a tier of the cultivation arc. Within each world are multiple **regions** of increasing difficulty — tougher enemies, better drops, and greater risk.

Unlocking a region also opens it for [[Worlds/Gathering|Gathering]] and [[Worlds/Mining|Mining]].

---

## Overview

- **Idle/automated**: assign character to a region → they fight and loot continuously
- Combat runs via [[Combat]] mechanics (auto-attack + equipped [[Secret Techniques]])
- Each region has a **minimum realm** to enter and a **power range** for its enemies
- Regions unlock sequentially within a world — clear one to open the next
- A world's first region unlocks automatically when the player reaches its minimum realm

## UI Flow

1. Player opens the Worlds screen → stays on the **World** tab
2. Each region shows its enemy types and notable drops
3. Clicking **Assign** on an unlocked region navigates to the **Combat screen** with that region's enemies
4. Enemies are drawn from the region's enemy list (random pick each fight)
5. Clicking **← Back** returns to the Worlds screen

## The Six Worlds

---

### World 1 — The Mortal Lands
*Realms: Tempered Body → True Element*

The settled edges of the cultivation world — training grounds, wilderness, and minor spirit zones. Enemies are human cultivators, beast packs, and low-grade demonic animals. Drops are modest but consistent.

| Region                      | Min Realm               | Enemies                                      | Notable Drops                            |
| --------------------------- | ----------------------- | -------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| Outer Sect Training Grounds | Tempered Body L1        | Outer sect disciples, sparring dummies       | Common materials, gold                   |
| Borderland Wilds            | Tempered Body L5        | Wolf packs, bandit scouts, wandering beasts  | Common herbs, Black Tortoise Iron        |
| Qi-Vein Ravines             | Qi Transformation Early | Qi-sensing beasts, rogue disciples           | Uncommon herbs, Crimson Flame Crystal    |
| Misty Spirit Forest         | Qi Transformation Peak  | Forest spirits, awakened tree demons         | Uncommon materials, Beast Cores          |
| Heaven's Edge Peak          | True Element Early      | Sky beasts, thunder hawks, rogue cultivators | Rare herbs, Void Stone fragments         |
| Thunderstorm Plateau        | True Element Peak       | Lightning wyrms, storm elementals            | Rare materials, Origin Crystal fragments |

### World 2 — The Ancient Frontier
*Realms: Separation & Reunion → Immortal Ascension*

Ruins of a lost civilisation swallowed by the wilds. Enemies are stronger and more varied — ancient war constructs, spirit beasts with elemental cores, and wandering ascension-level cultivators. First world where death is a real threat.

| Region | Min Realm | Enemies | Notable Drops |
|---|---|---|---|
| Shattered Sky Desert | Separation & Reunion 1st | Sand dragons, bone constructs, desert wraiths | Rare herbs, Mithril Essence |
| Demon Beast Plains | Separation & Reunion 3rd | Herd-hunting beasts, elemental boars | Rare minerals, Beast Cores |
| Sunken Immortal City | Immortal Ascension 1st | City guardian constructs, trapped immortal shades | Rare–Epic materials, artefact fragments |
| Primal Qi Wastes | Immortal Ascension 2nd | Qi-devoured beasts, corrupted cultivators | Epic herbs, Origin Crystals |
| Blood Sea Periphery | Immortal Ascension 3rd | Blood sea leviathans, qi-blood mutants | Epic materials, Dragon Saliva Grass |

### World 3 — The Forbidden Lands

Ancient battlefields and sealed grounds where Saint-level beings clashed. The air itself is saturated with lingering killing intent. Enemies hit significantly harder — elemental and soul attacks become common. [[Secret Techniques]] are essential here.

| Region | Min Realm | Enemies | Notable Drops |
|---|---|---|---|
| Saint Burial Grounds | Saint Early | Burial guardians, remnant Saint corpse-soldiers | Epic herbs, Star Metal Ore |
| Primal Qi Wastes (Deep) | Saint Middle | Qi-storm elementals, ancient beast kings | Epic materials, Purple Cloud Vine |
| Void Rift Expanse | Saint Late | Void-touched cultivators, spatial rift predators | Epic–Legendary herbs, Skyfire Meteorite |
| Nine-Death Mountain Range | Saint King 1st | Peak-Saint rogue elders, mountain demon lords | Legendary material fragments, Technique scrolls |
| Sealed War Altar | Saint King 3rd | Ancient construct commanders, bound war spirits | Legendary materials, rare Law books |

|---|---|---|---|
| Origin Qi Spring Depths | Origin Returning 1st | Origin-spring guardians, crystallised qi constructs | Legendary herbs, Heavenly Profound Metal shards |
| World Root Caverns | Origin Returning 2nd | Root-merged beasts, cavern elder demons | Legendary minerals, Heaven Spirit Dew |
| Primordial Forest Core | Origin King 1st | Ancient wood sovereigns, forest origin spirits | Immortal Revival Leaf, Elemental Essence Beads |
| Heaven Beast Sanctuary | Origin King 2nd | Origin King-grade beasts (territorial) | Legendary materials, soul-type Technique scrolls |
| Ancient Origin Altar | Origin King 3rd | Awakened origin constructs, bound ancient cultivators | High-tier artefact fragments, Legendary law books |

*Realms: Void King → Emperor Realm*

The boundary between the mortal world and the upper heavens. Space fractures. Enemies are void-born creatures, ascended beasts, and Dao-comprehending rogue emperors. Every fight is a life-or-death engagement. Artefact refining materials are richest here.

| Region | Min Realm | Enemies | Notable Drops |
|---|---|---|---|
| Fractured Space Corridors | Void King 1st | Space-fracture predators, void elementals | Void Stone, Skyfire Meteorite |
| Void Sea Shores | Void King 3rd | Void sea leviathans, drifting war spirits | Heavenly Profound Metal, Elemental Essence Beads |
| Dao Inscription Ruins | Dao Source 1st | Dao-inscription guardians, petrified Dao cultivators | Heaven Spirit Dew, Emperor artefact fragments |
| Source Peak Summits | Dao Source 3rd | Peak Dao Source ascetics, elemental sovereigns | Legendary Technique scrolls, Law books |
| Ancient Emperor Tomb | Emperor Realm 1st | Tomb guardians, Emperor Realm remnant wills | Emperor-grade artefact fragments |
| Heaven Sword Ridge | Emperor Realm 3rd | Sword Dao spirits, fallen Emperor cultivators | Rare Law books, top-tier artefact cores |

### World 6 — The Open Heaven
*Realms: Half-Step Open Heaven → Open Heaven Layer 6*

The threshold of true immortality. Few cultivators ever reach this world. Enemies are Open Heaven–grade beings: spatial lords, cosmic beasts, and immortal remnants. Drops here are the rarest in the game.

| Celestial Rift Expanse | Open Heaven L2 | Rift predators, spatial sovereign beasts            | Esoteric materials, spatial law fragments       |
| Eternal Storm Arena    | Open Heaven L3 | Storm sovereigns, tempest-born constructs           | High-rank Open Heaven drops                     |
| Cosmic Beast Grounds   | Open Heaven L4 | Mid-rank Open Heaven beasts (territorial)           | Peak materials                                  |
| Heaven's Core          | Open Heaven L6 | Ancient Open Heaven cultivators, cosmic apex beasts | Rarest drops in the game                        |

## Region Unlock

- The first region of a world unlocks when the player reaches its minimum realm
- Each subsequent region within the same world requires clearing the previous one
- "Clearing" = killing a target number of enemies **or** defeating the region boss (TBD)
- Unlocked regions persist permanently (TBD: through reincarnation)

---

## Death

- Enemies have a power level drawn from a normal distribution centred on the region's difficulty
- Outlier enemies can kill an undergeared character → sent back to the last safely farmed region
- Death penalty beyond the setback: 10% of current QI progression on current minor realm
